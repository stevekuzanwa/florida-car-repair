MOT TESTING

FLORIDA CAR Repair provide a full MOT test service, and can also carry out repairs should your vehicle fail. We charge £29.95 for an MOT, and should your vehicle fail the test FLORIDA CAR Repair can provide you with a full quotation and repair to get your car back on the road. No matter where you carry out your repairs we will provide a re-test free of charge within 10 days of the original MOT date.

An MOT test is an annual requirement for most vehicles on the roads once they are three (or more) years old.
Arrange an appointment Online and have an MOT test completed up to one month before the expiry of your MOT certificate. Service your car at FLORIDA CAR Repair at the same time as your MOT, with trained technicians and the latest diagnostic equipment – saving you money and time, and making an inconvenient Service a little more convenient.
